About

Who We Are

Lancaster Safety Consulting, Inc. (LSCI) is a B2B occupational safety consultancy based in Wexford, PA. We partner with companies that need to achieve and maintain OSHA compliance — providing the training, written programs, audits, and ongoing support to keep their workforce safe and their operations inspection-ready.

Services

What We Do

We offer a full range of safety services under annual consulting partnerships, scaled to fit your organization's size and risk profile.

OSHA Compliance Partnerships

Ongoing compliance support, regulatory guidance, and inspection readiness for companies of all sizes.

Safety Training

OSHA 10/30-hour, forklift certification, NFPA 70E electrical safety, and additional industry-specific courses.

Mock OSHA Inspections

Simulate a real OSHA inspection to identify gaps before an actual inspector does. Walk away with a prioritized action plan.

Written Safety Programs

Custom, regulation-ready written safety programs tailored to your operations and hazard profile.

Online Training (LMS)

Scalable online training delivery through our learning management system — trackable, recordable, and audit-ready.

Safety Audits

Comprehensive site audits assessing safety culture, documentation, physical conditions, and regulatory alignment.

Who We Serve

Our Clients

We work with companies in industrial, manufacturing, aerospace, HVAC and trades, government, and commercial real estate — typically organizations with 30 or more employees that need structured OSHA compliance support but don't have a dedicated full-time safety director.
